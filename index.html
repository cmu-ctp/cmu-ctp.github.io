CMU Center for Transformational Play

We are a research group at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. Our mission is to bring together world-class expertise from across the university to design, build, and study transformational games.

Learn more about us

Our Four Pillars

Transformational play consists of games and playful experiences that change how people think, feel, and behave.

Community

We bring together interdisciplinary stakeholders from CMU and beyond who care about the power of play to change lives.

Research

We study the impact of transformational games and synthesize existing research to drive innovation in game design.

Design

We combine rigorous, research-backed theory and excellence in craft to make high quality transformational games and playful experiences.

Impact

We ensure our games reach the people who need them through our partners and networks.
